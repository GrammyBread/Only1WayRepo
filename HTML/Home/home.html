Only One Way To Burn It Down

What is it?

Is a free science fiction eBook written and illustrated by a single author.

The book is written in script format, includes character art, and emphasizes descriptive visuals which mirror television/animated shows.

The entire 4 part series will be free FOREVER and is available for reading on both mobile and desktop devices through this site.

How often is it released?

Sections for this series will be released on a weekly basis until November of 2022.

The sections are approximately 15-20 pages in length.

A precise release calendar can be found on my release schedule page. Likewise, you can find a table of contents for the current part (Part 1) on my google drive.
