Ela Alanas of Altas-Minas is a land-born, rust-water mutant solene from the neglected outer system of Helio-Altas. When a tragedy results in her exile, she enlists in the Soli fleet– Hoping for a second chance to do good in her life, and for the chance at avenging her dying-species by upending the tyranny she believes is consuming them all…

Zak Ilceaus-Zacel of is an out-of-bond, station born sel from the inner system station of Breva. When a shocking revelation results in his forced military recruitment, his life and self-perception are flipped upside down. He must muster the will to save himself from placement upon Exile's front-line, all while coming to terms with what lies below the skin…
