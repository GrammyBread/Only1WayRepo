Ela Alanas of Altas-Minas is a female soli (solene). She is a land-born, rust-water mutant from the neglected outer system of Helio-Altas. Though young of age and full of potential, her difficult life compelled her to join the Soli fleet in hopes of gaining status, and a chance to save her dying-species from the evil she believes is consuming them…

Zak Ilceaus-Zacel of Ismas is a male soli (sel). He is an out-of-bond, station born from the inner system station of Breva. When a shocking revelation results in his forced military recruitment, his life and self-perception are flipped upside down. He must muster the will to save himself from placement upon Exile's front-line, all while coming to terms with what lies below the skin…
